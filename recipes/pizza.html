Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a classic american italian quizine made of dough, tomato sauce, and cheese

mix flour and water in a bowl and mix together until it wont drip onto the floor. Then, knead in activated yeast and leave it out for an hour. afterwards, roll it out, add tomato sauce and cheese on top, and bake at 400 deg fahrenheit for ~20 mins

home

ingredients

  • Dough
  • activated yeast
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

steps

  1. mix in water and flour to bowl
  2. knead dough
  3. add activated yeast
  4. knead dough some more
  5. let dough sit on counter or in fridge for 1 hour
  6. take it out and put it onto cutting board
  7. roll it out or press down into flat circle
  8. add sauce and then cheese
  9. heat up oven to 400
  10. put pizza in oven and cook for 20 mins
