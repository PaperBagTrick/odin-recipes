Spagetti Recipe

Spagetti is a classic american italian quizine made of noodles and any sauce, typically marinara sauce

take a large pot and fill it with water, the more the better. Once the water is boiling, add the noodles, and stir until noodles are not too soft but not still hard. This is typically called al dente. Strain the pasta over a sink using a strainer, then add sauce to pot. once the sauce is hot, you can add the pasta back to pot, and your done.
