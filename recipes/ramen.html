Ramen Recipe

ramen is an easy meal, typically for broke college students

boil 2 cups of water in a small pot. once its boiling, add the noodles. once noodles are broken apart, add sauce, powder, or other ingredients. If you are including any meat, cook beforehand. If you are using any vegetables, boil them in water, then once they are cooked through, add the ramen

home

ingredients

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • flavoring packet

steps

  1. boil 2 cups of water to pot
  2. add noodles
  3. wait for 2 minutes roughly
  4. add sauce and powder
